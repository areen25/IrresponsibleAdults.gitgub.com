About Us

We're not just another crew – we're a family of adrenaline junkies, master criminals, and speed demons who live for the thrill. Crew Code

We keep it simple – just a few rules to make sure everyone has a blast:

  • Respect all crew members – we're family here
  • No griefing other crew members during activities

  • Help newer members learn the ropes
  • Keep the drama out – save it for the NPCs
  • Have fun and embrace the chaos!
